1. article 存储元数据(既要导出的数据) (unique index: md5, primary key: id)
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|    字段名   |   类型    	|  说明          						                                |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|    id       |   integer       | primary key                                                       |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| category_id |   smallint      | article category                                                  |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| channel_name|  varchar(12)     | domain name                                                      |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|   url       |   varchar(250)  | article url                                                       |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|   title     | varchchar(150)  | article title                                                     | 
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|   pub_date  |   timestamp(0)  | article created on                                                |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|   author    | varchar(12)     | article author                                                    |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|comment_count| integer         | article comment count                                             |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|view_count   | integer         | article view  count                                               |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  brief      | smallinteger    | sex                                                               |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|word_count   | smallinteger    | zone                                                              |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  industry_id|   varchchar(12) | industry                                                          |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  created_on |   varchchar(12) | industry                                                          |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|   url_md5   |   char(32)      | url hash value                                                    |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|content_md5  |   char(32)      | content hash value                                                |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|page_type    | smallint        | whether has comment in comment table, default 0                   |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|status       | smallint        | use for scale,default 0                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

2. article_content 文章内容表 (unique index : article_id)
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| article_id  |  integer        | foreign key                                                       |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| content     |  text           | article content                                                   |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

3. article_matched_keywords 文章匹配的关键字表 (index: (keyword, created_on, status), index: article_id, primary key: id)

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| id          | integer         |    primary key                                                    |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| article_id  | integer         |   article id                                                      |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|pub_date     | timestamp(0)    |   article created on                                              |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| keyword     | varchar(12)     |   matched keyword                                                 |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  emotion    |   smallint      | emotion                                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| status      | smallint        | use for scale,default 0                                           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

4. comment 评论表 (index: md5, primary key: id)

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  id          |   integer       | primary key                                                      |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  article_id  |   integer       | article id                                                       |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  url         |   varchar(250)  | comment url                                                      |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  md5         |   char(32)      | url hash value                                                   |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  floor       |   integer       | floor number                                                     |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  created_on  |   timestamp(0)  | comment created on                                               |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  author      |   varchar(12)   | comment author                                                   |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  content     |   text          | comment content                                                  |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  status      |   smallint      | use for scale,default 0                                          |
+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
